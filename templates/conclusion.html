Conclusion

Having prior knowledge of how to clean, organize, and loop through data in Python made building the machine learning models easier than we thought it would be. In addition, we were able to build visualizations to engineer the machine learning features and validate the predictions more easily in Tableau than in D3 or Plotly. Although our model results ended up being fairly mediocre, we are clearly on the right track and with some some additional feature engineering and fine tuning of the model we could have a fairly reliable forecasting algorithm.
